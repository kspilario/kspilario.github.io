## COURSE DESCRIPTION
Analytical and numerical solutions of ordinary differential equations (ODE). Introduction to partial differential equations (PDE). Applications in chemical engineering.

## COURSE CREDIT
3 units (2 hr/week lec, 3 hr/week lab)

## COURSE OBJECTIVES
After completing this course, the students should be able to:
* Solve initial value and boundary value problems involving ordinary differential equations analytically;
* Discuss the different forms of ordinary and partial differential equations, as encountered in modeling physical phenomena; and,
* Calculate the approximate solutions of ordinary and partial differential equations using computer-aided numerical methods.
